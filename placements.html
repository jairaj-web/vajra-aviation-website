How Our Placement Works

A structured 4-step journey from classroom to career.

1

Structured Training

DGCA/EASA-aligned courses taught by Ex-IAF instructors with military-grade discipline and industry focus.

2

Exam Preparation

Mock tests, question banks, and one-on-one coaching ensure first-attempt success in DGCA and EASA examinations.

3

Industry Connect

Resume building, interview preparation, and direct introductions to our airline and MRO hiring partners.

4

Job Placement

Our dedicated placement cell coordinates interviews, follows up with employers, and supports you until offer letter in hand.
