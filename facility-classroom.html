What We Provide

Classroom Features

Our classrooms are equipped with the latest technology and resources to ensure an effective and engaging learning experience.

01

Digital Learning Tools

Smart boards, projectors, and digital displays for interactive learning. Students have access to online study materials, practice tests, and live aviation databases.

02

Ergonomic Seating

Comfortable, ergonomically designed seating arrangements that promote focus and reduce fatigue during long study sessions with optimal desk space for all materials.

03

Audio-Visual Systems

High-quality audio systems for clear instruction delivery. Video playback capabilities for aviation training films, ATC recordings, and simulation videos.

04

Library & Study Area

Dedicated library with 1000+ aviation textbooks, DGCA study materials, EASA manuals, and reference books. Quiet study zones for focused exam preparation.

05

Computer Lab

Computer lab with aviation software, flight planning tools, and online examination practice platforms for DGCA and EASA mock tests in a real exam environment.

06

Small Batch Sizes

We maintain small batches to ensure personalised attention for every student, allowing instructors to address individual learning needs with focused, direct guidance.
