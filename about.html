Our Story

Built by Warriors,
Trained for the Skies

Vajra Aviation Private Limited was founded in 2014 by decorated veterans of the Indian Air Force, who believed that military-grade discipline, precision and operational excellence could be the foundation of India's finest aviation training institute. What started as a vision became Bangalore's most trusted aviation academy.

Our founder, Gp Capt (Retd) Sunil Kumar, served over two decades in the IAF before establishing Vajra Aviation with a singular purpose: to bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world aviation standards. Every course, every module and every exam is crafted with the rigor that the aviation industry demands.

Today, Vajra Aviation is an ISO 9001:2015 certified institution, a proud partner of Aviotrace Swiss for EASA Part 66 training, and an affiliate of IANZL (International Aviation Academy of New Zealand). Our graduates fly with IndiGo, Air India, SpiceJet and work across India's premier MRO facilities.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide world-class aviation training that empowers every student with the knowledge, skills and military-grade discipline to excel in the global aviation industry. We are committed to delivering quality education through experienced Ex-IAF faculty, industry-aligned curriculum and internationally recognized certifications that meet DGCA and EASA standards.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted and globally respected aviation training institution — recognized for producing highly competent, disciplined aviation professionals who lead the growth and safety of civil aviation worldwide. We envision a Vajra graduate in every cockpit, every control tower and every maintenance hangar in the country.
