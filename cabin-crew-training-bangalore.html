What is the difference between cabin crew and air hostess?
Cabin crew is the gender-neutral term for all flight attendants — male and female — responsible for passenger safety and service on board. Air hostess is the traditional term used specifically for female flight attendants. The roles, training, and responsibilities are identical. Both terms are used interchangeably in India and by airlines worldwide.
