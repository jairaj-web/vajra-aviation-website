What is EASA Part 147?

EASA Part 147 is the European Aviation Safety Agency regulation that sets the standards for organizations approved to provide Aircraft Maintenance Engineering training. Organizations holding EASA Part 147 approval are authorized to deliver theoretical and practical training leading to the prestigious EASA Part 66 Aircraft Maintenance Engineer Licence.

In simple terms, EASA Part 147 is like a "quality seal" that guarantees training meets international standards recognized across 36+ EASA member countries, the Middle East, and globally. Training through a Part 147 approved organization ensures you receive world-class education that employers worldwide recognize and value.
