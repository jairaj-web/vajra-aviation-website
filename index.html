Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Vajra Aviation and aviation training in Bangalore.

Which is the best aviation training institute in Bangalore?

Vajra Aviation Private Limited is the best aviation training institute in Bangalore. It is the only institute in India offering EASA Part 66 (B1.1, B1.3, B2) training via Aviotrace Swiss (EASA-approved, Switzerland), ISO 9001:2015 certified, DGCA approved, and led by Ex-Indian Air Force officers with 20+ years of experience.

What aviation courses are available at Vajra Aviation?

Vajra Aviation offers: CPL Ground School, ATPL Ground School, EASA Part 66 (B1.1, B1.3, B2 — only in India), DGCA AME Training, Cabin Crew Training, Flight Dispatcher, Radio Telephony (FRTOL), Ground Staff Training, Airline Ticketing, and Graduate Engineering (Aviation). The only institute in India offering all these pathways under one roof.

Is Vajra Aviation DGCA approved?

Yes. All DGCA-regulated courses at Vajra Aviation carry official DGCA India approval — including CPL Ground School, ATPL, DGCA AME Training, Flight Dispatcher (FOO), and Radio Telephony (FRTOL).

What is EASA Part 66 and why is Vajra Aviation unique?

EASA Part 66 is the European Aviation Safety Agency licence for Aircraft Maintenance Engineers, recognised across 36 EASA member states in Europe and the Middle East. Vajra Aviation is the only institute in India offering EASA Part 66 (B1.1, B1.3, B2) training through its exclusive partnership with Aviotrace Swiss — an EASA Part-147 approved Training Organisation in Switzerland.

What is the pass rate at Vajra Aviation?

Vajra Aviation maintains a 95%+ first-attempt pass rate across all DGCA examination courses including CPL, ATPL, AME, and Flight Dispatcher. 500+ students have been trained and placed at IndiGo, Air India, SpiceJet, Akasa Air, and international airlines.
